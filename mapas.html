Explore the Battlefields of Valorant

Each map in Valorant offers a unique strategic layout, creative design, and multiple approaches for attackers and defenders. Below are some of the most iconic battlegrounds.

Bind

Located in Rabat, Morocco, Bind has no middle lane. Instead, it features two teleporters that enable fast rotations and surprise attacks.

Ascent

Set in Venice, Italy, Ascent is an open map centered around a large courtyard. Mid control is crucial for both teams to dominate the round.

Haven

Beneath a forgotten monastery, a clamour emerges from rival Agents clashing to control three sites. There‚Äôs more territory to control, but defenders can use the extra real estate for aggressive pushes.

Sunset

Sunset, located in Los Angeles, offers a balanced mix of long sightlines and close-quarter combat, with a traditional three-lane layout.

Lotus

Set among ancient ruins in India, Lotus introduces rotating doors and multiple routes for unpredictable gameplay.

Icebox

Icebox is located somewhere in the Arctic. The two planting sites are protected by both snow and metal, and are difficult to access horizontally. Take advantage of the ziplines and they'll never see you coming.

Fracture

A top-secret research facility divided by a failed radianite experiment. With defense options as divided as the map itself, the choice is yours.

Split

If you want to go far, you must climb. A pair of sites divided by an elevated center allows for rapid movement via two rope lifts. Each site has a large tower vital for control. Remember to look up before everything goes up in the air.

Breeze

Enjoy the sights of historic ruins or seaside caves in this tropical paradise. But bring something to protect yourself from the sun... and long-range gunfire. You'll need it in these wide-open spaces. Watch your flanks and feel the breeze.

Pearl

Attackers advance toward defenders in this two-site map set in an underwater city. Pearl is an environment-focused map with no mechanics. Take the battle to a compact Midfield or to more expansive areas in our first map on Omega Earth.

This is the worst map in the game, a fact.

Corrode

A walled French citadel converted into a radianite salt mine. Lay siege to the medieval streets using repurposed industrial machinery to advance through the layers of defense on this traditional three-lane map.
