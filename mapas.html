Explore the Battlefields of Valorant

Each map in Valorant offers a unique strategic layout, creative design, and multiple approaches for attackers and defenders. Below are some of the most iconic battlegrounds.

Bind

Located in Rabat, Morocco, Bind has no middle lane. Instead, it features two teleporters that enable fast rotations and surprise attacks.

Ascent

Set in Venice, Italy, Ascent is an open map centered around a large courtyard. Mid control is crucial for both teams to dominate the round.

Sunset

Sunset, located in Los Angeles, offers a balanced mix of long sightlines and close-quarter combat, with a traditional three-lane layout.

Lotus

Set among ancient ruins in India, Lotus introduces rotating doors and multiple routes for unpredictable gameplay.
