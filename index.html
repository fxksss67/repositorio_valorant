VETO

Veto is a sentinel from Senegal who, thanks to a genetic mutation, defies the rules of combat by nullifying his opponents' powers and technology. When Veto enters the battlefield, weapons are your only allies.

Abilities

-Leash:

EQUIP a sticky fragment of your mutation. FIRE to throw it. The fragment deploys upon impact and creates a trap that immobilizes enemies. Immobilized enemies are deafened and weakened. Enemies can destroy the trap before it activates.

-Shortcut:

EQUIP a vortex. FIRE to place it on the ground. While facing the vortex and within range, REACTIVATE the ability to teleport to its location. During the BUY PHASE, the vortex can be retrieved to be REUSED.

-Interceptor:

EQUIP the Interceptor. FIRE to place it at the targeted location. Once placed, REUSE the ability to activate it. Once activated, it will destroy any utility that can BOUNCE off a player or be normally destroyed by gunfire. Enemies can destroy the Interceptor.

-Evolution:

Instantly begins a full mutation, granting a combat boost, regeneration, and IMMUNITY to all forms of debilitation.

NEW MODE: SKIRMISH

A simplified combat experience now available in custom matches! Fast rounds, small maps, flexible loadouts, and short matches!

-Fast-paced:

Quick rounds and transitions, designed for high replayability.

-Absolute Precision:

No economy system, no default abilities (they can be enabled in the cheats menu). Keep your weapons or change them each round according to your strategy.

-Round Structure:

The first team to reach 10 kills wins. Unlimited round time, but victory conditions can be modified in the cheats menu.

-Flexible Team Size:

Play from 1v1 up to 5v5, with the option to form asymmetric teams.
