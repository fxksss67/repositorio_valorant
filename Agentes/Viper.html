Overview

Viper, the American chemist, deploys a full arsenal of poisonous chemical devices to control the battlefield and cripple her enemies. Her toxic screens and clouds let her dominate vision and space like no other agent.

  • SNAKE BITE: Equip a chemical launcher. Fire to launch a canister that shatters upon hitting the floor, creating a lingering chemical zone that damages and slows enemies.
  • POISON CLOUD: Equip a gas emitter. Fire to throw the emitter that you can reactivate to create a toxic gas cloud at the cost of fuel. The emitter can be picked up to be redeployed.
  • TOXIC SCREEN: Equip a gas emitter launcher. Fire to deploy a long line of emitters that you can reactivate to create a tall wall of toxic gas that obscures vision and damages enemies crossing through.
