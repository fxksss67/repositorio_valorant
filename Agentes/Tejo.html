Overview

A veteran intelligence consultant from Colombia, Tejo's ballistic guidance system pressures the enemy to relinquish their ground - or their lives. His targeted strikes keep opponents off balance and under his heel.

  • GUIDED SALVO: EQUIP an AR targeting system. FIRE to select up to two target locations on the map.
  • Stealth Drone: EQUIP a stealth drone. FIRE to throw the drone forward, assuming direct control of its movement.
  • Special Delivery: EQUIP a sticky grenade. FIRE to launch. The grenade sticks to the first surface it hits and explodes, Concussing any targets caught in the blast.
  • Armageddon (Ultimate): EQUIP a tactical strike targeting map. FIRE to select the origin point of the strike. FIRE again to set the end point and launch the attack, unleashing a wave of explosions along the strike path.
