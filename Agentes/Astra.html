Overview

Ghanaian Agent Astra harnesses the energies of the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With her astral form and star placements, she controls every engagement, manipulating enemies and allies alike from the stars above.

  • GRAVITY WELL: Place a star in Astral Form. Activate it to create a gravity well that pulls players toward the center before it explodes, making all trapped players vulnerable.
  • NOVA PULSE: Place a star in Astral Form. Activate it to detonate a concussive blast that briefly stuns all players caught inside.
  • NEBULA / DISSIPATE: Place a star in Astral Form. Activate it to transform the star into a Nebula smoke. Use Dissipate to temporarily remove a star from the map, returning it for future use.
  • ASTRAL FORM / COSMIC DIVIDE (Ultimate): Activate your Ultimate to enter Astral Form and place stars across the map. When Cosmic Divide is charged, use it to create an enormous wall that blocks bullets and dampens audio.
