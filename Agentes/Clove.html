Overview

Scottish troublemaker Clove manipulates life and death to keep fights going their way. Whether alive or dead, Clove uses their powers to outsmart enemies, confuse attackers, and give their team the upper hand.

  • MEDDLE: EQUIP a fragment of immortal essence. FIRE to throw it, applying a decay effect to all players hit, weakening them temporarily.
  • RUSE: EQUIP to enter a map view and set cloud locations. FIRE to create lingering clouds that block vision. ALT FIRE to confirm multiple clouds at once.
  • PICK-ME-UP: Absorb the life force of a fallen enemy Clove helped kill to gain a temporary speed boost and overheal.
