Overview

Veto is a sentinel from Senegal who, empowered by a rare genetic mutation, disrupts the rules of combat by nullifying his enemies‚Äô powers and technology. When Veto steps onto the battlefield, only pure skill and gunplay remain.

  • LEASH: EQUIP a sticky fragment of your mutation. FIRE to throw it. Upon impact, it deploys and creates a trap that immobilizes enemies caught within. Immobilized enemies are deafened and weakened. The trap can be destroyed before activation.
  • SHORTCUT: EQUIP a vortex. FIRE to place it on the ground. While facing the vortex and within its range, REACTIVATE to teleport to its position. During the BUY PHASE, the vortex can be retrieved for reuse.
