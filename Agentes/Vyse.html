Overview

Vyse is a sentinel from Venice who manipulates magnetic energy to reshape the battlefield. Her control over metal allows her to fortify positions, block vision, and disable enemy technology with precision and style.

  • MAGNET SNARE: EQUIP a magnetic snare. FIRE to throw it. Upon impact, it attaches to a surface and activates when an enemy passes nearby, pulling them briefly and dealing minor damage. Enemies can destroy the snare before activation.
  • FERRO FIELD: INSTANTLY deploy a magnetic dome. ACTIVATE it to create a field that blocks bullets and distorts vision. Allies inside gain slight movement speed while enemies are slowed.
  • METAL SURGE: EQUIP a surge of magnetic shards. FIRE to send them forward, disabling enemy traps and utility in their path. The shards can also temporarily block enemy radar detection.
  • IRON VORTEX (Ultimate): INSTANTLY create a massive magnetic storm that covers a large area. Enemies caught inside are pulled toward the center and highlighted through walls for Vyse and her team.
