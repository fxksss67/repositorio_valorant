Overview

Well-dressed and well-armed, French weapons designer Chamber expels precision and style. He leverages his custom arsenal to hold the line and pick off enemies from afar with deadly accuracy.

  • TRADEMARK (C): EQUIP a trap that scans for enemies. When a visible enemy comes in range, it destabilizes the ground around them, creating a lingering field that slows players caught inside.
  • HEADHUNTER (Q): ACTIVATE to equip a heavy pistol. ALT FIRE with the pistol to aim down sights for more precision.
  • RENDEZVOUS (E): EQUIP a teleport anchor. While on the ground and in range of the anchor, REACTIVATE to quickly teleport to it. The anchor can be picked up and redeployed.
  • TOUR DE FORCE (Ultimate - X): ACTIVATE to summon a powerful custom sniper rifle that will kill an enemy with any direct hit. Killing an enemy creates a lingering field that slows players caught inside.
