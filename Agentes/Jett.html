Overview

Representing her home country of South Korea, Jett's agile and evasive fighting style lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies before they even know what hit them.

  • CLOUDBURST: INSTANTLY throw a projectile that expands into a brief vision-blocking cloud on impact with a surface. HOLD the ability key to curve the smoke in the direction of your crosshair.
  • UPDRAFT: INSTANTLY propel Jett high into the air.
  • TAILWIND: ACTIVATE to prepare a gust of wind for a limited time. RE-USE the wind to propel Jett in the direction she is moving.
  • BLADE STORM (Ultimate): EQUIP a set of highly accurate throwing knives. FIRE to throw a single knife and recharge knives on a kill.
